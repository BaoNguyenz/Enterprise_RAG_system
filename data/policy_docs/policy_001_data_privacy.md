# Data Privacy and Protection Policy
**Document Type:** Policy
**Policy ID:** POL-001
**Owner:** Chief Privacy Officer (CPO)
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
**Review Date:** 2025-01-01
**Regulation:** GDPR, CCPA

## Purpose
This policy establishes TechDocs Inc.'s commitment to protecting personal data and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection regulations including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, vendors, and third-party service providers who process personal data on behalf of TechDocs Inc.

## Stakeholders
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Chief Privacy Officer (CPO) | Policy ownership, regulatory liaison |
| Data Protection Officer (DPO) | GDPR compliance, subject rights requests |
| IT Security Team | Technical safeguards, breach detection |
| Legal Department | Contract review, regulatory filing |
| All Employees | Compliance with policy, incident reporting |

## Data Categories and Retention
| Category | Examples | Retention Period | Legal Basis |
|---------|---------|-----------------|-------------|
| Account Data | Name, email, password hash | Duration of account + 2 years | Contract |
| Usage Data | Logs, search queries | 90 days | Legitimate interest |
| Payment Data | Billing address, last 4 digits | 7 years | Legal obligation |
| Marketing Data | Email preferences | Until opt-out | Consent |

## User Rights (GDPR)
Users have the right to: access, rectification, erasure (right to be forgotten), portability, and objection to processing. Requests must be fulfilled within 30 days.

## Data Breach Procedure
1. Detect and contain breach within 1 hour
2. Notify DPO and IT Security immediately
3. Report to supervisory authority within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
4. Notify affected users within 7 days if risk is high
5. Document incident in breach register (REG-BREACH-001)
