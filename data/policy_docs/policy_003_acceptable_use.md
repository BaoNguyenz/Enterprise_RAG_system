# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
**Document Type:** Policy
**Policy ID:** POL-003
**Owner:** IT Department / Legal
**Effective Date:** 2024-03-01
**Applies To:** All employees, contractors, interns

## Purpose
This policy defines acceptable use of TechDocs Inc. systems, networks, software, and data assets.

## Stakeholders
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| IT Department | Enforce technical controls, monitor usage |
| HR Department | Disciplinary actions for violations |
| Legal Department | Policy review, legal risk assessment |
| Managers | Communicate policy to direct reports |
| All Users | Acknowledge and comply with AUP |

## Permitted Uses
- Business-related communications and productivity
- Accessing SaaS tools approved by IT (see approved list APP-LIST-2024)
- Reasonable personal use during breaks (not impacting performance)
- Participation in approved professional development

## Prohibited Activities
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Installation of unlicensed or unapproved software
- Sharing credentials with third parties
- Using company systems for personal business ventures
- Accessing, downloading, or distributing copyrighted material without authorization
- Circumventing security controls or monitoring systems
- Cryptocurrency mining on company hardware

## AI Tool Usage
Employees may use approved AI tools (see POL-008 AI Usage Policy). Prohibited:
- Entering confidential company data into public AI systems (e.g., public ChatGPT)
- Using AI to generate code that bypasses security controls
- Relying on AI output without human review for customer-facing content

## Monitoring
TechDocs reserves the right to monitor all activity on company systems. Monitoring is conducted in compliance with applicable laws and communicated to employees upon hiring.

## Violations
Violations may result in:
- Warning and remedial training (minor, first offense)
- Suspension of system access (repeated or moderate violations)
- Termination and legal action (severe violations)
