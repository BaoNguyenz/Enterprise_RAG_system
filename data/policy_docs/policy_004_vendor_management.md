# Third-Party Vendor Management Policy
**Document Type:** Policy
**Policy ID:** POL-004
**Owner:** Procurement / Legal
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
**Regulation:** ISO 27001 A.15, SOC 2

## Purpose
Govern the selection, onboarding, monitoring, and offboarding of third-party vendors who access or process TechDocs Inc. data or systems.

## Stakeholders
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Procurement Team | Vendor sourcing, contract negotiation |
| Legal Department | Contract review, NDA, DPA execution |
| IT Security Team | Security assessment, access provisioning |
| DPO | Data processing agreement review |
| Finance Department | Invoice verification, spend management |
| Business Unit Owners | Vendor performance monitoring |

## Vendor Classification
| Tier | Risk Level | Examples | Review Frequency |
|------|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| Tier 1 — Critical | High | Cloud providers, payment processors | Quarterly |
| Tier 2 — Important | Medium | SaaS tools, analytics vendors | Bi-annual |
| Tier 3 — Standard | Low | Office supplies, facilities | Annual |

## Onboarding Requirements
### Tier 1 Vendors Must Provide:
- SOC 2 Type II report (within 12 months)
- ISO 27001 certificate or equivalent
- Signed Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- Penetration test results (within 12 months)
- Business Continuity Plan summary

### All Vendors Must Sign:
- Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA-STD-2024)
- Acceptable Use Policy acknowledgment
- Data Processing Agreement (if processing personal data)
