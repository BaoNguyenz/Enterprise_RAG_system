# TechDocs Support Plans — Specification
**Document Type:** Product Specification
**Product ID:** TDSUPPORT-2024
**Category:** Support Services

## Overview
TechDocs offers four tiers of support to match different business needs, from community forums to dedicated technical account management.

## Support Tier Comparison
| Feature | Community | Standard | Premium | Enterprise |
|---------|-----------|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Price** | Free | Included with Pro | $299/month | Custom |
| **Channels** | Forum only | Email | Email + Chat | Phone + Slack |
| **Response Time (P1)** | N/A | 24 hours | 4 hours | 1 hour |
| **Response Time (P2)** | N/A | 48 hours | 8 hours | 4 hours |
| **Uptime SLA** | None | 99.5% | 99.9% | 99.95% |
| **Named Support Engineer** | No | No | No | Yes |
| **Technical Account Manager** | No | No | No | Yes |
| **Onboarding Assistance** | No | Docs only | 2 sessions | Unlimited |
| **Training** | Self-serve | Webinars | Custom workshops | On-site available |
| **Quarterly Reviews** | No | No | No | Yes |

## Incident Severity Definitions
| Severity | Definition | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| P1 Critical | Service completely down, data loss | Production outage |
| P2 High | Major feature unavailable | Search not returning results |
| P3 Medium | Feature degraded, workaround exists | Slow export performance |
| P4 Low | Minor issue, cosmetic | UI display bug |

## SLA Credits
If TechDocs fails to meet uptime SLA, customers receive service credits:
- 99.0–99.5% uptime → 10% monthly credit
- 95.0–99.0% uptime → 25% monthly credit
- < 95.0% uptime → 50% monthly credit
